Service


Sea shipping. Fast and easy mode of transport.

Baltic hill managament is the one-stop supplier for your crew, part of our office "crew" worked in various positions on board in international shipping, through our short lines and clear communication, international independent offices, decades of experience in the sector, our crew answer your requirements, whether it be total or partial crew management, temporary or permanent employment, we give all situations the same attention.
